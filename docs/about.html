Our vision

We believe that sustainable, long-term relationships between people and organizations will help drive social change and benefit communities.

The Growesty platform

Our platform connects individuals to the causes they care about in their local area, resulting in greater engagement and support for community organizations such as non-profits (NPOs), non-governmental organizations (NGOs), and other social initiatives such as animal rescues and shelters.

By driving engagement with such organizations, Growesty supports individuals and organizations who want to make a difference and create social change. Growesty itself is a responsive, user-friendly, and intuitive to use web platform with the goal of supporting and augmenting natural discovery processes for people to connect to their interests, causes, organizations, and greater community.

Growesty and you

Whether you are looking to volunteer, make a donation, find a cause to champion, work for an NGO or NPO, attend events, get to know your area better, or simply meet new, like-minded people and have fun, you can find what you are looking for.
